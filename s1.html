Genetic testing and analysis

We offer genetic testing services, which include DNA sequencing, genotyping, and analysis of genetic data. We have revolutionized healthcare and personalized medicine, providing valuable information for individuals to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.

There are numerous mutations that have been identified in the human population on Earth. Mutations are changes in the DNA sequence that can occur naturally or be induced by various factors such as environmental exposures or errors during DNA replication.
